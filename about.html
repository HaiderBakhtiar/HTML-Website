About Us

Wang Wang Chinese Restaurant is located in Zarak Plaza, across from City Tower on University Road in Peshawar, KPK, Pakistan. This restaurant offers a wide range of authentic Chinese dishes. From classics like Sweet and Sour Chicken to more unique options, there's something for everyone. Wang Wang provides a friendly atmosphere for families, friends, or couples looking for a delicious meal. If you're in Peshawar and craving Chinese food, visit Wang Wang for a taste of China in Pakistan.
